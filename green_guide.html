A sneak peak into this weeks newsletter, going back to basics…10 Easy care tips!

1) Feel the soil to know how often to water.
2) It is better to underwater than overwater.
3) Skip the fertilisation if you are unsure.
4) Stability, finding the right spot is key.
5) Smaller plants are the fastest growing.
6) Low light plants work well in the bathroom.
7) Water deeper, not lightly and frequently.
8) Prune your plants. Give them a trim!
9) Make sure you pots have drainage holes.
10) Have a spring clean, dust your plants!
