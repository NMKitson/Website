Loving plants is one thing, keeping them alive is another...and where the journey of Plant Envy began!


I do not know about you, but it seems only natural when you get a new house you get a new plant, or two, then the collection really starts to grow, before you know it you have a jungle.


Even with some TLC, in our house it seemed potluck if they survived, a topic that came up amongst friends, where we all agreed we could kill a cactus between us!


Over a glass of vino, before we knew it the challenge was set, at the time I'm not sure we knew what the challenge was but we accepted.


From then on there was some friendly competition, lots of plant spam and a new level of plant chat and Plant Envy was born.


Then we invited the nation of Plant lovers to join our little community and the rest is history as they say!
